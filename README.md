## How to Play

1. **Slice** - Swipe across objects to slice them
2. **Avoid Bombs** - Don't slice the bombs or it's game over
3. **Use Shield** - Click the shield button to protect yourself once per game
4. **Score High** - Chain combos and trigger frenzies for maximum points
5. **Play Again** - Click "Play Again" to restart
